butternut squash mac and cheese

So the squash mixed with the cheese is wonderful, creamy, cheesy, and the garlic type of sauce adds a light sweetness .

The key to a delicious squash mac and cheese lies in the balance of flavors and texture like sweet, cheesy.

- my auntie brandie

Ingredients:

  • peeled and cubed butternut squash: 1 large or 2 small squash 1 lb
  • vegetable broth or chicken broth : 1 and 1/4 cups
  • milk: 1 and 1/4 cups
  • :
  • cloves of garlic, minced : 3
  • uncooked small/medium pasta shells :1 pound
  • roughly chopped kale, stems discarded :1-2 cups(70-140g)
  • plain greek yogurt : 1/3 cups(80g)
  • salt :1 teaspoon
  • freshly ground black pepper :1/2 teaspoon
  • small pinch ground nutmeg :20g
  • shredded cheese sharp white or cheddar :2 cups (225g)

Preparation:

  1. combine the squash, broth,milk, garlic in a medium saucepan.
  2. preheat oven to 375ºF(191ºC).
  3. boil enough water for the pasta .
  4. pour the warm butternut squash mixture into a blender or food processor.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 add the pasta and kale to the sauce and stir until combined then it will soak up and then pour it all into a 1 9x13 inch baking pan, 12 inch oven safe skillet.
  • 1bake, covered in aluminum foil for 20 minutes then remove the foil and bake for 5 minutes .
